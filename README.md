# WinTUS - Residency Exam Companion

WinTUS is a comprehensive iOS application designed to assist medical students and doctors in Turkey preparing for the Medical Specialization Examination (TUS).

## Features

- **Score Calculation:** Calculate your estimated TUS score using official ÖSYM formulas and historical data (Standard Deviation & Mean).
- **Residency Guide:** Explore residency programs, quotas, and minimum scores from previous exam periods (sourced from ÖSYM guides).
- **Trial Exam Tracking:** Record and track your progress in trial exams.
- **Study Timer:** specialized timer to manage your study sessions effectively.
